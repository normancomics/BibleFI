## The Solution: Biblical DeFi Architecture

### Core Platform Features

**1. Real-Time Tithing Streams**
Using Superfluid's streaming protocol, believers can set up continuous tithing flows to churches worldwide. No more forgetting to tithe or managing monthly transfers—your generosity flows automatically, just as Scripture calls for consistent giving.

*"Each one must give as he has decided in his heart, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver." - 2 Corinthians 9:7*

**2. Biblical Wisdom-Guided DeFi**
Our AI-powered biblical advisor analyzes over 2,000 Scripture references to money, wealth, and stewardship, providing personalized financial guidance that aligns with biblical principles. Users earn "wisdom scores" for applying scriptural teachings to their investment decisions.

**3. Faith-Based Yield Strategies**
- **Parable of the Talents Farming**: Multi-token yield strategies inspired by Jesus's teaching on faithful stewardship
- **Joseph's Seven Years Protocol**: Automated savings strategies based on storing grain for lean times
- **Solomon's Wisdom Staking**: Conservative, wisdom-guided staking pools with risk management

**4. Farcaster Native Experience**
Built as a mini-app directly within Farcaster, Bible.fi enables believers to share financial wisdom, cast about their stewardship journey, and engage with the faith-tech community without leaving their social feed.
